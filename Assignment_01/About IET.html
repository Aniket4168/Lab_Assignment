About the Institute of Engineering & Technology

Launched on 04 September 1996 with a humble intake of 90 students, IET-DAVV has evolved into a beacon for aspiring engineers. From its inception, it blended academic rigor with practical experience to create leaders in technology and innovation.

Today, IET-DAVV offers extensive undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, backed by robust research initiatives and dynamic industry collaborations. Our vibrant campus life, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a curriculum that adapts to global trends set us apart.
